# Ad Creative Requirements (Meta/Facebook Ads)

## Overview

This document outlines the technical requirements and best practices for creating Meta/Facebook ad creatives.

---

## Media Requirements

### Image/Video Format Requirements

**Critical Requirement:**
- Your ad requires at least **three images** in **SQUARE FORMAT**
- Your ad must include at least **one image or video**

### Media Selection Options

1. **Select images** - Upload existing images
2. **Select videos** - Upload existing videos
3. **Create video** - Use Meta's video creation tools

### Optimize Creative for Each Person

**Advantage+ Creative**
- Meta will test ad creative and destination based on each person's likelihood to respond
- See [possible enhancements](https://www.facebook.com/business/help/advertising)

---

## Ad Copy Components

### 1. Primary Text ⓘ

**Purpose:** Tell people what your ad is about

**Best Practices:**
- Lead with the pain point or value proposition
- Keep it concise (under 125 characters for mobile optimization)
- Use emojis strategically to draw attention
- Front-load the most important information
- Include social proof where relevant

**Character Limits:**
- Recommended: 125 characters (displays fully on mobile)
- Maximum: 500 characters (text will be truncated with "...See More")

---

### 2. Headline ⓘ

**Purpose:** Write a short headline

**Best Practices:**
- Clear and direct
- Action-oriented
- Match the promise in primary text
- Test variations

**Character Limits:**
- Recommended: 40 characters
- Maximum: 255 characters (but will be cut off in most placements)

---

### 3. Description ⓘ

**Purpose:** Include additional details

**Best Practices:**
- Provide supporting information
- Reinforce the value proposition
- Not shown in all placements (News Feed often hides this)

**Character Limits:**
- Maximum: 30 characters (very limited - use wisely)

---

### 4. Website URL ⓘ

**Format:** `http://www.example.com/page`

**Purpose:** Enter the website URL for your ad

**Requirements:**
- Full URL including `https://`
- Must be a valid, accessible URL
- Should match the promise/offer in the ad

**Advanced Option:**
- [Build a URL parameter](https://www.facebook.com/business/help/1016122818401732) - Add UTM tracking

---

### 5. Display Link ⓘ

**Purpose:** Enter the link you want to show on your ad

**Best Practices:**
- Keep it clean and readable (e.g., `endlessreply.com/demo`)
- Remove unnecessary parameters
- Can be different from actual destination URL

---

### 6. Call to Action ⓘ

**Default Options:**
- Learn More
- Sign Up
- Download
- Book Now
- Get Quote
- Apply Now
- Contact Us
- Get Offer
- Shop Now
- Subscribe
- (and more)

**Advanced:**
- **Add another option** - Create multiple CTA buttons for testing

## Technical Specifications

### Image Specs

| Placement | Recommended Size | Aspect Ratio | File Type | Max Size |
|-----------|-----------------|--------------|-----------|----------|
| Feed | 1080 x 1080 px | 1:1 (Square) | JPG, PNG | 30 MB |
| Stories | 1080 x 1920 px | 9:16 (Vertical) | JPG, PNG | 30 MB |
| Reels | 1080 x 1920 px | 9:16 (Vertical) | JPG, PNG | 30 MB |

**Text Overlay Recommendation:**
- Keep text in image under 20% for optimal delivery
- Meta no longer penalizes text-heavy images, but performance may suffer

---

### Video Specs

| Placement | Recommended Size | Aspect Ratio | Length | File Type | Max Size |
|-----------|-----------------|--------------|--------|-----------|----------|
| Feed | 1080 x 1080 px | 1:1 (Square) | 1 sec - 241 min | MP4, MOV | 4 GB |
| Stories | 1080 x 1920 px | 9:16 (Vertical) | 1 sec - 2 min | MP4, MOV | 4 GB |
| Reels | 1080 x 1920 px | 9:16 (Vertical) | 3 sec - 90 sec | MP4, MOV | 4 GB |

**Video Requirements:**
- H.264 compression
- Square pixels
- Fixed frame rate
- Stereo AAC audio compression, 128kbps+

**Best Practices:**
- Add captions (85% of video watched on mute)
- Hook in first 3 seconds
- Keep under 15 seconds for optimal completion rate

## Quality Checklist

Before launching, ensure:

- [ ] At least 3 square images uploaded (1:1 ratio)
- [ ] Primary text is under 125 characters
- [ ] Headline is under 40 characters
- [ ] All links are tested and working
- [ ] UTM parameters are added to destination URL
- [ ] Display link is clean and branded
- [ ] CTA button matches the offer
- [ ] Images/videos are high resolution (1080px minimum)
- [ ] Text overlay on images is minimal
- [ ] Video has captions
- [ ] Ad previewed across all placements
